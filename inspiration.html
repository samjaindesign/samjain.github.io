Recipie of the week: Chef Vineet Bhatia: Creamy Pumpkin Soup


PUMPKIN SOUP INGREDIENTS
  • 1kg pumpkin/butternut squash, cut into Â¾ inch cubes
  • 1/2 onion, sliced (white, brown, yellow)
  • 3 garlic cloves , sliced
  • 3 cups (750ml) vegetable or chicken broth/stock , low sodium
  • 1 cup (250 ml) water
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1.5 teaspoon chilli powder
  • Finish touch: 1/2 - 3/4 cup (100-125 ml) cream , half and half or milk

Process:

Cut the pumpkin into 3cm / 2.25" slices. Cut the skin off and scrape seeds out (video is helpful). Cut into 4cm / 1.5" chunks. Place the pumpkin, onion, garlic, broth and water in a pot - liquid won't quite cover all the pumpkin. Bring to a boil, uncovered, then reduce heat and let simmer rapidly until pumpkin is tender (check with butter knife) - about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and use a stick blender to blend until smooth (Note 3 for blender). Season to taste with salt and pepper, stir through cream (never boil soup after adding soup, cream will split). Ladle soup into bowls, drizzle over a bit of cream, sprinkle with pepper and parsley if desired. Serve with crusty bread!!!
