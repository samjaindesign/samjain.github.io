Our Menu

Our menu is divided into three sections: appetizer, entree, and dessert. Our appetizers are inspired by Indian food market street vendor menu styles. The items on the menu vary from bhaji which is crispy deep-fried dollops of spicy chickpea batter served with spicy condiments called chutney to Samosas. Samosas contain a small amount of spicy potato or meat filling that is then wrapped in dough and deep-fried until crispy and flaky. Our Entree dishes are heavy curry-based with sauce ranging from tomato, and yogurt/milk-based curries. Our dessert contains delicious pudding, mouth-watering cakes, and regional sweets dipped in sugar syrup. Check out our menu for more options.
